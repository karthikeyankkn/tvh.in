1) CSR

Overview

TVH firmly believes in contributing to society so as to uplift the underprivileged and give them a fair chance in life. To this end, TVH has involved itself wholeheartedly in corporate social initiatives.

Medical Camps

Medical camps are routinely organized at TVH sites for contract laborers and their family members.

Creches & Schools

Creches and schools have also been organized at each site to educate the children of contract laborers. Only qualified teachers are employed at these creeches and schools.

Child Adoption

TVH has adopted more than 700 orphan children from the Indian Council of Child Welfare. TVH also donates to Udavum Karangal, a charity for orphans and the destitute, as well as to other such charities.

Rural Initiatives

Under the ‘Give Life Project’, TVH periodically conducts medical camps in economically backward villages, and organizes day care facilities for the children of the laborers.

2) TVH – Projects I

Address:

Lumbini Square Little Children – Day Care Centre
Purasawalkam, Chennai 600 007.

Vision

Objectives:
  • Health awareness
  • Activity based learning
  • Personality Development
  • Environmental awareness
  • Spread awareness of the complexities that cause children to be physically exploited and vulnerable.

The Project

The students were enrolled by the end of October after many orientation sessions with their parents. The project began on November 5, 2007. The school building was inaugurated by Mr. Ravichandran, Chairman TVH. Regular classes for students are conducted in English. Various techniques like activity base learning using charts, spell board, building blocks, counting beads, drawing, craft works, rhymes, games and drill are conducted.

Periodical medical check-ups are conducted for the students,and medicines are provided for their ailments.National days and festivals are also celebrated in the school. The Parents Teacher’s healthy relationship is also helping to improve the quality of students.teachers also aid the mothers who share their problems with them.

TVH – Projects II

Address:

Medical Camp at Alamathi village,
Ponneri Taluk in Thiruvallur District on 5th January 2008

Vision:

To provide medical assistance to prenatal and postnatal mothers, and children through regular medical checkups. Ensure nutritional needs for prenatal and postnatal mothers by providing them nutritional packages.

To educate pregnant mothers through regular workshops on aspects to be followed during pregnancy. Educate mothers to understand the special nutritional needs of new born, notably the value of breastfeeding and the importance of introducing suitable complementary foods at the right age.

Protecting children from infection, by such measures as immunization against common childhood diseases and provision of safe water and sanitation. Ensuring that children receive quality care when they fall ill.

Reason for Working in the Village:

Malnutrition is prevalent in the village among women and children. The major factors contributing to it are their food patterns, lack of nutritional awareness, lack of awareness of the facilities available around them, and their accessibility towards them and occupational pattern. Anemia is also largely present among women.

Services:

Medical Check-ups: Through the project, a general medical check-up assessing weight, BP, Hemoglobin, diabetics, malnutrition and deficiency will be organized.

Feedback:

  • Through the four months intervention in the village the following outcomes were received.
  • A good rapport has been established in the village
  • The nutrition packages have increased the health status of the beneficiaries
  • The beneficiaries awareness of various health aspects has increased
  • The participation level of the beneficiaries has increased.
